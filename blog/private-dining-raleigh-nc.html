Private Dining in Raleigh NC

Published March 17, 2026

Private events at Crawford & Son aren't about renting a space — they're about having Chef Scott Crawford and his team focus entirely on your night. The exposed brick walls, the candlelit intimacy, the low-light attention to detail that defines the restaurant — all of it becomes yours. Forty-five seats. All of them facing the moment you came here to create.

The menu is custom. Chef Scott doesn't pull the regular menu and call it private dining. He works with you on what you want the night to taste like. Maybe it starts with those Manchego-herb biscuits everyone remembers. Maybe it builds toward a Wagyu short rib braised until it falls apart. Maybe the wine comes in pours that Jordan Joseph, our beverage director, has thought about specifically for you. The full bar is available for cocktails — he can create something from the regular list or build something new just for this event.

We handle rehearsal dinners often. The night before the wedding, when the family's gathered and the week's finally caught up with itself. The restaurant becomes a gathering place. The food is good enough to not overshadow the conversation, but thoughtful enough that people notice. The service is there when you need it, quiet when you don't. The historic Oakwood location means guests can walk the neighborhood after — there's real neighborhood feel, not just a restaurant in an office park.

We've hosted corporate dinners. Birthday celebrations. Small weddings. The kind of intimate gatherings where the space matters as much as the food. Exposed brick softens electric atmosphere. Candles do most of the heavy lifting. The kitchen shows up. There's no rushing, no feeling like the next reservation's circling.

For custom menu planning and private event inquiries, reach out to Kat Rider. She handles the details — the timing, the flow, the way your night feels when the team's all focused on it. The events page has more information, but a conversation is really where it starts. Tell her what matters. Tell her what you want people to remember about this night.

Crawford & Son on Person Street in historic Oakwood. Candlelit. Intimate. Built for the gatherings that mean something. Let us be part of it.
