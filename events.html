Private Events

Your gathering, our kitchen, our home

Intimate Celebrations at Crawford & Son

Our dining room in historic Oakwood is built for occasions that matter. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, closing a deal, or marking a season of life with people you love, Crawford & Son offers a backdrop that feels intentional and personal—low-lit, candlelit, and unhurried.

We believe private events should feel like they're yours, not someone else's template. That's why we work directly with Chef Scott Crawford and Jordan Joseph to build menus and beverage programs tailored to your vision and season.

What You Get

Custom Menus

Chef Scott Crawford works with seasonal ingredients and a product-driven approach. Your menu is designed for your event—not pulled from a binder of options.

Dedicated Service

Our team focuses entirely on your event. Thoughtful timing, attentive service, and the kind of care that makes guests feel genuinely welcomed.

Full Beverage Program

Craft cocktails crafted by Jordan Joseph, or work with our sommelier to pair wines that complement your menu. Beer and spirits round out the full bar.

Types of Events

Rehearsal Dinners

The night before the wedding should feel like yours. A private space, Chef Scott's seasonal cooking, and enough time to breathe with the people who matter most.

Corporate Dinners

Close deals and build culture over dinner. Our intimate setting encourages real conversation, and our team handles the details so you can focus on what matters.

Birthday Celebrations

Mark the occasion with people who've shaped your life. Custom menu, a cozy room lit by candles and exposed brick—the perfect stage for toasts and memories.

Anniversary Dinners

Whether it's one year or fifty, celebrate the milestone in a space designed for intimacy. Chef Scott's food tastes like care and attention.

Holiday Gatherings

Bring your team, your family, your chosen community. We'll create a seasonal menu that feels festive and grounded, served with intention.

The Setting

Our dining room is intimate by design. Candlelit tables, exposed brick walls, and dark furniture create an atmosphere that feels thoughtful and grown-up—the kind of space where conversations matter and moments stick with you.

Located on Person Street in historic Oakwood, Crawford & Son is walkable, accessible, and feels like a real destination. Your guests arrive knowing they're somewhere special.
