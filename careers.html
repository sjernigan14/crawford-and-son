Careers

Crawford & Son opened its doors in November 2016, born from Chef Scott Crawford's vision of creating a restaurant that honors both family and community. The name itself is a tribute – a nod to the deep connections that define us, and the relationships that make us who we are.

2018: Recognized as Triangle's Restaurant of the Year
2019: Jolie opened, bringing the family's Paris experience back to Raleigh, expanding our commitment to fine dining and authentic hospitality

Our Mission

We believe in the power of authentic connections and meaningful relationships. Our ever-evolving menu reflects the seasons and our community, crafted with intention and served with warm hospitality. We're not just a restaurant – we're a gathering place where every guest feels valued and every team member is part of something greater.

Our Guiding Values

Integrity

We do what we say we'll do, with honesty and transparency in every interaction.

Respect

We honor our guests, our team, our ingredients, and the communities we serve.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every detail, from the kitchen to the dining room.

Elevating Others

We lift up those around us, celebrating their growth and potential.

Winning Work Ethic

We bring energy, dedication, and heart to everything we do.

Embracing Change

We adapt, innovate, and grow, always staying true to our core mission.
