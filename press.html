Awards & Recognition

James Beard Award Semifinalist

Chef Scott Crawford has been named a James Beard Award semifinalist multiple years, recognized for his approach to seasonal, product-driven cooking and his impact on the Raleigh dining scene.

2018 Triangle's Restaurant of the Year

Recognized by the Triangle community for excellence in cooking, service, and hospitality since opening on Person Street in historic Oakwood.

Recognition for the thoughtful, welcoming service and team culture that defines the Crawford & Son dining experience.
